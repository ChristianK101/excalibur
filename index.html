Our Story

Where Legends
Are Made

Nestled in the heart of San Diego, Excalibur Cigar & Scotch Lounge is more than a place to smoke — it's a sanctuary. A family-owned establishment where every guest is welcomed like a regular and every visit becomes a memory.

We are California's only official Montecristo Cigar Lounge, home to over 15,000 premium cigars from around the world, a wall of rare Scotch and spirits, and a full bar stocked with craft beers and fine wines.
